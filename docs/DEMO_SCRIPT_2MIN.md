# PrepSmart - 2 Minute Demo Script

**Total Time**: ~1 minute 50 seconds

---

## Opening (15 seconds)

"Hi, I'm presenting **PrepSmart** - an AI-powered crisis preparedness assistant that creates personalized survival plans in under 90 seconds.

Right now, people face two major crises: natural disasters like hurricanes and economic emergencies - 900,000 federal workers were just furloughed. Existing solutions are overwhelming 50-page PDFs or expensive $500 survival kits that nobody uses."

---

## The Solution (20 seconds)

"PrepSmart uses **6 specialized AI agents** working in parallel to create personalized plans based on your location, budget, and situation.

It's **transparent** - you watch the agents work in real-time.
It's **fast** - complete plans in 90 seconds.
It's **budget-conscious** - three tiers from $50 to $200, with free alternatives.
And it costs just 7 cents per plan to run."

---

## Live Demo (60 seconds)

**[Screen share - show the live app]**

"Let me show you. Here's the live demo at [show URL].

**[Click Get Started]**

I'll select **Economic Crisis** since that's urgent right now.

**[Fill form quickly while talking]**
- Location: Washington DC - where many furloughed workers are
- Household: 2 adults, 1 child
- Income: $0 - just lost my job
- Budget: $100

**[Submit and show agent dashboard]**

Watch the agents work:
- **Risk Assessment** is analyzing the situation
- **Financial Advisor** is building a 30-day survival plan
- **Supply Planning** is creating a food stockpiling list
- **Resource Locator** is finding local food banks and legal aid in DC
- **Video Curator** is finding how-to videos for unemployment filing

**[Show results as they come in - if not ready, have pre-recorded]**

Here's the output:
- Daily action checklist for 30 days
- $2,480 in monthly benefits I'm eligible for
- Supply list optimized for $100
- 4 local resources in DC with addresses
- Educational videos

**[Click Download PDF]**

One click downloads a complete survival guide."

---

## Unique Value (15 seconds)

"What makes PrepSmart different:
- **Dual-mode**: Natural disasters AND economic crises
- **Transparent multi-agent**: You see exactly what's happening
- **Evidence-based**: All recommendations from FEMA, CDC, and Red Cross
- **Production ready**: Live on Azure, 95%+ success rate"

---

## Technical Highlights (15 seconds)

"Built with Python, Flask, Claude 3.5 Sonnet, and a custom blackboard pattern for multi-agent coordination. Complete test coverage with Playwright. Deployed on Azure Container Apps. Open source and ready to help people right now."

---

## Closing (5 seconds)

"PrepSmart - because everyone deserves a plan. Thank you!"

## Tips for Delivery

1. **Practice with a timer** - aim for 1:50 to have buffer
2. **Pre-load the demo** - have it open in browser
3. **Have backup screenshots** in case live demo fails
4. **Speak clearly and pace yourself** - don't rush
5. **Emphasize impact**: "Can help 900K+ workers RIGHT NOW"
6. **Show confidence** - you built something real and deployed

---

## If Demo Breaks (Backup Plan)

Have these screenshots ready:
1. Homepage
2. Agent dashboard showing all 6 agents working
3. Complete results page with all sections
4. PDF download

Say: "Let me show you screenshots from a recent run" and walk through them in 30 seconds.
